Exploring Abha

The capital of Asir Province, Abha is situated in the Aseer mountain region, Saudi Arabia at an elevation of 2200 m. The city is situated at the southern end of the Red Sea and is popular for panoramic views, rocky mountains, mud-walled Shada Palace, lakes and theme parks

Jabal Thera – Or the Green Mountain
Aseer National Park – An exciting family attraction
Abu Kheyal Park – For breathtaking views
Shamsan Fort – A remain of the Ottoman history
