Saudi Arabia "Al-ahsa"

jeddah is a city located in the western region of Saudi Arabia, on the coast of the Red Sea. It is the second-largest city in Saudi Arabia, with a population of over 3.5 million people. Jeddah is known for its rich history and culture, as well as its modern infrastructure and vibrant economy. The city has been an important trading hub for centuries, connecting Asia, Africa, and Europe through its port. class="mb-3"

The city's architecture reflects its diverse cultural influences, with traditional Arabian-style buildings alongside modern skyscrapers. Jeddah is also home to several museums and cultural centers that showcase the city's history and heritage.
