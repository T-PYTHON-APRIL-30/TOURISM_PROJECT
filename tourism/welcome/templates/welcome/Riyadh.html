Saudi Arabia "Riyadh"

Riyadh is the capital city of Saudi Arabia and is located in the central region of the country. It is the largest city in Saudi Arabia and has a population of over 7 million people. The city is known for its modern architecture, luxurious shopping malls, and high-end restaurants. Riyadh is also home to many historical sites such as the Masmak Fortress, which played a significant role in the unification of Saudi Arabia. The city has a desert climate with hot summers and mild winters. It is also an important economic hub with many international businesses having their headquarters in Riyadh. The King Khalid International Airport serves as a major gateway to the city, connecting it to other parts of the world.
