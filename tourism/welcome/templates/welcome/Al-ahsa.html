Saudi Arabia "Al-ahsa"

Al-Ahsa is a region located in the eastern province of Saudi Arabia. It is known for its rich history, culture, and natural resources. The region has a population of over 1 million people and covers an area of approximately 33,000 square kilometers. Al-Ahsa is home to several important historical sites, including the Al-Qarah Mountain and the Al-Ahsa Oasis. class="mb-3"

The region's economy is primarily based on agriculture, with date palm cultivation being a major industry. Al-Ahsa dates are considered some of the best in the world and are exported globally. The region also has significant oil reserves, which contribute to its economy.
