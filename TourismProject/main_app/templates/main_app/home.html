Saudi Arabia is an incredible country that has been open to tourists since 2019. It is best known for being home to two of the most sacred cities in Islam, Makkah and Madinah. Here are some reasons why you should consider visiting Saudi Arabia:

  1. A Spiritual Sanctuary: Two of Islam’s holiest cities are situated in Saudi Arabia: Mecca and Medina, which are revered by over 1 billion Muslims.
  2. Mesmerizing Pre-Islamic History: Saudi Arabia is home to some of the most fascinating discoveries that changed the face of history.
  3. Endless Diversity: From the Red Sea coast to the mountains of Asir, Saudi Arabia is a land of contrasts and diversity.
  4. Awe-Inspiring Scuba Diving: The Red Sea is one of the best places in the world for scuba diving.
  5. A Gastronomical Paradise: Traditional Arabic food is one of the most loved and revered cuisines in the world.
