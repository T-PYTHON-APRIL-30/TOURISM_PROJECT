Abha city is characterized by many natural, historical and shopping landmarks. One of the most famous natural landmarks in the city is Al-Soudah Mountain and Al-Akhdar Mountain and Aseer National Park. One of the most important historical landmarks in the city is Shamsan Castle and Al-Habalah Suspended Village. One of the most prominent shopping landmarks in the city is Abha Mall.

Tourism in Saudi Arabia is diverse and includes the most attractive and active religious tourism due to the presence of the Two Holy Mosques, environmental tourism, business tourism, as well as shopping tourism which enjoys a significant position in terms of attraction.
